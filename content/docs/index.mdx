# Welcome to ElizaOS

**ElizaOS** is a cutting-edge framework for building autonomous AI agents that can think, remember,
and interact naturally across multiple platforms. Whether you're creating chatbots, autonomous
agents, business process handlers, or interactive NPCs, ElizaOS provides the tools and
infrastructure to bring your AI vision to life.
